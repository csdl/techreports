Future Directions

 

An area that this research does not look at is the difference between collaboration in the small and collaboration in the large. Also the difference between a static hypertext reader like Mosaic and AEN is not investigated. AEN dynamically generates many navigation aids that Mosaic cannot at this time. What is the effect of these navigation aids on the reader and author of hypertext?

Kona SRS Questions

These questions are intended to guide the development of the Kona Software Requirements Specification (SRS) Document.

  1. What is the intended purpose of the SRS?
  2. Who are the intended users of Kona?
  3. Who will participate in the requirements elicitation process?
  4. What is the "business case" for the development of Kona?
  5. What are the characteristics of the various communities of users of Kona?
  6. What are the problems confronted by the users that Kona will solve?
  7. What is the "wish list" of the various user communities for Kona?
  8. What are the most important functional requirements for Kona?

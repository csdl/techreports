ICS CourseApp Registration Page

To receive your ICS CourseApp account, fill out the following information and supply your ITS (uhunix) account name. 

If you do not have an ITS account, then you cannot register using this page. If you are a continuing UH student, you must obtain an ITS account (using the ITS account request form) before you can proceed with CourseApp registration. 

If you are a new UH student, then you should send email to uhmics@hawaii.edu explaining your situation.  After confirming that you are not a continuing student, you will be manually registered in CourseApp. 

After you press the "Submit" button, the system will check the data you entered. If valid, then an ICS CourseApp password will be mailed to your ITS account. Once you have received this password, you can use it to log in to the CourseApp system.  Check your email in about five minutes. 

If you have previously requested an ICS CourseApp password but forgot it, just re-register here.   A new password will be emailed to you. Your current course request information will not be erased.

Duration

Starting in the fall of 1994, 12 students in the graduate seminar ICS 613 are using AEN. The students are using AEN to learn about designing and implementing collaborative systems using Egret. The experiment started in September 1994 and is continuing through December 1994. The duration corresponds to the Fall semester. During this period the contents of the hypertext document will be incrementally constructed while the system is in use.
